Women of Color who DJ

Representation in DJing


Being a disc jockey is an important role that many people take on in the world of entertainment. When it comes down to having the right music and vibe for an event, it's important to select someone that knows how to keep the energy flowing the entire time. We often see and hear of many male DJs taking on this role. It can be argued that DJing is a male dominated field, and even though there are women who are tasked with being the disc jockey, there are even fewer numbers of WOC (women of color) in the industry.
How can we further support WOC in the industry?

Below is a list of Female DJs who are shaking up the game. Check them out and show some support!
